MANYUKO, aka Jonathan Weinreich, is a musician, music producer,<br/>songwriter and multi-instrumentalist based in Tel Aviv. Having grown up <br/>in New York, MANYUKO's music is inspired by a wide variety of musical<br/>styles and cultures, as well as his experience in producing and <br/>composing for artists, theater and film scores. His music blends together warm acoustic <br/>instrumentals with electronic, industrial and organic <br/>sounds, and straightforward lyrics.<br/><br/>
MANYUKO is currently working on his debut album under the artist name JUNO VINE.<br/>Written, composed, produced and recorded by him, <br/>the album will also integrate collaborations with artists from around the<br/>world, including dancers, choreographers, filmmakers and more.
